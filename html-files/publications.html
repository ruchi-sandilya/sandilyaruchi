Ruchi Sandilya


Welcome to my website! I am a researcher in numerical analysis and scientific computing. In particular, I work on numerical solutions of partial differential equations (PDEs) and optimal control problems governed by PDEs. I am also interested in working on data driven modeling based on computational intelligence and machine learning methods.
