Ruchi Sandilya

Welcome to my website! I am a postdoctoral researcher in numerical analysis and scientific computing. My research focus is on developing efficient learning-augmented controls for complex fluid flows with uncertain parameters. Flow control is important in many applications including improving the performance of planes or vehicles and design of energy-efficient buildings to meet national energy and environmental challenges. Machine learning provides a mathematical framework that is ideal for learning the control laws. The main goal of my research is to advance the theory and practice of learning-augmented controls in complex flow problems.
